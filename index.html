How quickly can you start working on my project?

We typically begin new projects within 1-3 business days after receiving all necessary information and initial payment. For urgent projects, we offer expedited services - please mention your timeline when contacting us.

Yes, we offer various support packages depending on your needs. These can include technical support, content updates, security monitoring, and performance optimization. We'll discuss these options during the project planning phase.

Can you work with clients outside of Zimbabwe?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration and we use various communication tools to ensure smooth project management regardless of location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, PayPal, and mobile money payments. For international clients, we primarily use bank transfers and PayPal. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion.
